# IELTS-style Topics — 試験対策的なお題

> Phase 3 が安定してきたら。IELTS Writing Task 2 系の意見・議論の練習。
> いきなり 250 words 書かなくて OK。最初は 150 words くらいから、自分の意見を一段落で。

## ライティングの型（参考）

- **Position statement** — 自分の立場を 1 文で
- **Reason 1** — 理由 1 + 具体例
- **Reason 2 / Counter** — 理由 2 もしくは反対意見への目配り
- **Restatement** — 立場の言い直し（同じ言葉を繰り返さない）

---

## Opinion / Agree-Disagree 系

1. **"Working from home is better than working in an office."** To what extent do you agree or disagree?
2. **"Universities should focus on practical skills rather than academic theory."** Discuss.
3. **"Designers have a moral responsibility for how their products are used."** Agree or disagree?
4. **"Cities should be designed primarily for pedestrians, not cars."** Discuss.
5. **"Reading fiction is as valuable as reading non-fiction."** Discuss.
6. **"Social media has done more harm than good to public conversation."** Agree or disagree?
7. **"Lifelong learning is a personal duty, not just a personal choice."** Discuss.
8. **"Caring for others should be considered a kind of expertise."** Discuss.
9. **"Children should be taught creative writing as seriously as math."** Agree or disagree?
10. **"Art and design education prepare people for many jobs, not just creative ones."** Discuss.

## Discuss both views

11. **Some people believe technology makes us more connected; others say it isolates us.** Discuss both views and give your opinion.
12. **Some argue universities should accept students based on academic merit only; others say diversity should also matter.** Discuss both views.
13. **Some think organizations work best with clear hierarchies; others prefer flat structures.** Discuss both views.
14. **Some say happiness depends mostly on circumstances; others say it depends on attitude.** Discuss both views.
15. **Some think privacy is a fundamental right; others think transparency is more important in modern society.** Discuss both views.

## Problem / Solution

16. **Many people in cities report feeling lonely. What are the causes, and what can be done?**
17. **Knowledge work can lead to burnout. What are the causes, and how could workplaces respond?**
18. **People read fewer long books than before. Is this a problem? If so, what could be done?**
19. **Many design products quickly go out of date and become waste. What causes this, and what solutions might help?**
20. **In many countries, students learn English but struggle to speak it. What might be the causes, and what could help?**
